About Barbaariye Technology

Barbaariye Academy is a premier e-learning platform founded in 2021 by Barbaariye, the leading authority in online business and technology education in Somalia. With the mission to provide high-quality, accessible education in the Somali language, the academy has grown to become a beacon of knowledge and skills development for the Somali-speaking community.

Barbaariye Technology waa madal E-learning casri ah oo u heellan horumarinta waxbarashada Soomaaliya. Waxay bixisaa fursado waxbarasho oo tayo leh, iyadoo adeegsanaysa tiknoolajiyada si ay u gaarsiiso aqoon iyo xirfado casri ah dhalinyarada, ardayda, iyo xirfadlayaasha. Madalku waxay ujeedadeedu tahay in ay kor u qaaddo awoodda barashada online-ka, taasoo qof walba u suurtagelinaysa inuu si sahlan u barto xirfado cusub, korna ugu qaado aqoontiisa iyada oo aan xaddidnayn goobta uu joogo.

Barbaariye Technology waxay aaminsan tahay in tiknoolajiyadu tahay furaha horumarka, sidaas darteedna ay ka go’an tahay in ay dhalinyarada Soomaaliyeed ku xoojiso aqoon iyo xirfado ay kula tartamaan suuqa shaqada caalamiga ah.

Who is the founder of Barbaariye Technology?

Nasiib Qasim is a renowned entrepreneur and educator who leads the largest and hub YouTube channel dedicated to online business and technology in the Somali language. Since 2021, he has been actively blogging and mentoring in the fields of e-commerce, cryptocurrency, and artificial intelligence. Gelle’s journey into the online business world began in 2020 when he realized the significant gap in accessible education for Somalis. This inspired him to create a platform where individuals could learn and grow in their native language, ensuring a better understanding and application of the knowledge gained.
